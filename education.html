Senior Secondary Stage

Every Sunday, 63 rural students attend the Nisarg open-class education.

English and life skills essentials are covered in Ms. Sudha Gandhi's class.

Mathematics classes are instructed by Mr. Sanket Aggarwal.

Science is taught by other Ph.D. professors from the Happy2Care Foundation.

Every month, Ms. Shruti Oswal and team CHIP Nagpur volunteers, plan some creative fun activities to teach the children in an entertaining manner.

Undergraduate / Postgraduate Stage

Conservation efforts involving various college students, such as the construction of gabion structures and loose boulder structures, were planned. Additionally, these tasks involve maintaining plants, managing saplings from a forest nursery, and de-weeding.
