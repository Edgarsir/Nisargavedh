About Us

Our Story

NISARGAVEDH, meaning "Sanctuary of Nature" in Sanskrit, was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to protect and preserve our planet's precious ecosystems for future generations.

Since our establishment, we have been at the forefront of environmental conservation, working tirelessly to combat deforestation, protect endangered species, and promote sustainable practices in communities around the world.

Our dedicated team of scientists, activists, and volunteers work hand-in-hand with local communities to create lasting change. We believe that environmental protection and community development can go hand in hand.

Amazon Rainforest Initiative

Our flagship project focuses on protecting 500,000 hectares of pristine rainforest in the Amazon basin. Working with indigenous communities, we implement sustainable land management practices while combating illegal deforestation.

Location: Brazil, Peru, Ecuador | Started: 2018

Ocean Conservation Program

Protecting marine ecosystems through coral reef restoration, reducing ocean plastic pollution, and establishing marine protected areas. Our team works with coastal communities to promote sustainable fishing practices.

Wildlife Sanctuary Network

Establishing and maintaining wildlife sanctuaries to protect endangered species. Our sanctuaries provide safe havens for tigers, elephants, and countless other species while supporting local conservation efforts.

Wetland Restoration Project

What We Do

Our comprehensive approach to environmental conservation

Conservation

Protecting endangered species and preserving critical habitats through research-based conservation programs.

Reforestation

Planting native trees and restoring degraded forests to combat climate change and enhance biodiversity.

Education

Empowering communities through environmental education and awareness programs for sustainable living.
